Fried Rice

Background

This recipe is the most authentic recipe that has ever existed. The veggies perfectly fried, the rice perfectly fried, and the taste of soy sauce. It is a classic.

Recipe

    Ingredients
  • Frozen peas and carrots
  • Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • 1 Egg
    Directions
  1. Heat oil in pan over high heat
  2. Cook the frozen veggies until they are thawed out and warm.
  3. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, tossing to combine.
  4. Crack an egg into the pan and stir everything together to cook the egg.
  5. Serve immediately
